This is my github for now, where I will be uploading the data studio weekly projects.

It's been quite fun so far

Columbia, NYC, summer 2019

All projects are focused around the Greek reality of the recent years. Some have to do with the refugee crisis, others put under examination aspects of the national economy during the years of the financial crisis, while others emphasize into aspects of social co-existence. One of them aims to dig into how Greeks break off a marriage.

Data analysis has been done mostly using Python, pandas and libraries such as matplotlib, plotly, Altair, and others. Visualization mostly with Adobe Illustrator, QGIS, and Mapbox.
